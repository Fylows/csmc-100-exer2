iReady

iReady is a web-based application designed to assist Local Government Units (LGUs) in enhancing their recovery and response efforts during times of calamities. By integrating pre-storm relief planning and logistics optimization, iReady aims to improve community resilience and ensure that aid and resources are allocated efficiently and effectively before disasters strike.
